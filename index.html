        House rules
        Serious mechanics, unserious consequences
        Read before rage voting
        Accounts & aura balances
        • Each friend has an aura account with a starting balance of 1000 AURA.
        • Balances can go as low as your friendships can handle. Negative aura is allowed.
        • One member is “You” and controls this device. No login – this is a demo bank for drama only.
        Creating a proposal
        • Any member can propose to increase or decrease another member’s aura, including their own.
        • Every proposal must specify a title, a short description, a target, a type and an aura amount between 1 and 500.
        • Creating a proposal instantly stakes 100 AURA from the proposer’s balance.
        • You can only have one active proposal at a time; finish your current chaos before starting more.
        Voting eligibility & power
        • Members with < 200 aura can spectate but cannot vote – their aura is “too fragile”.
        • Members with 200+ aura can vote and their voting power depends on their balance when the proposal starts:
        • 200–499 aura → 0.5 voting power.
        • 500–799 aura → 1.0 voting power.
        • 800–1799 aura → 1.5 voting power.
        • 1800+ aura → 2.0 voting power.
        • Voting power is locked per proposal at creation time and does not change if balances move later.
        Thresholds & early decisions
        • At proposal creation, the app sums all eligible voting power; this is the locked total.
        • Each proposal needs 51% or more of the locked total to either approve or deny early.
        • If YES votes reach 51%+ of total power before 12 hours, the proposal is approved immediately.
        • If NO votes reach 51%+ of total power before 12 hours, the proposal is denied immediately.
        Outcomes & aura changes
        • Approved proposal:
        • Proposer’s 100 AURA stake is refunded.
        • If type is increase, the target gains the proposed aura amount.
        • If type is decrease, the target loses the proposed aura amount.
        • Denied proposal (51%+ NO) or expired proposal (12 hours, no 51% reached):
        • Proposer’s 100 AURA stake is burned into the void.
        • Target’s balance does not change at all.
        Voting behavior
        • Members can vote YES or NO and can change their vote while the proposal is still active.
        • The proposer is allowed to vote on their own proposal because of course they are.
        • The target can also vote on proposals about themselves; self-defense is encouraged.
        • Everyone can see all proposals, even if they currently cannot vote.
        This is not a real bank
        • No sign-up, no passwords, no real money, no blockchain, no leaderboard on your credit report.
        • All data lives only in this page while it is open; refreshing wipes the drama.
        • Use Aura Bank for group jokes, roast nights, and deciding who owes who emotional reparations.
